ABSTRACT 
i 
The Rehabilitation Unit Management System (RUMS) is a specialized software solution 
to support the operations and management of the rehabilitation unit within Amar Jyothi Charitable 
Trust, a voluntary organization providing inclusive education, medical care, vocational training, child 
guidance, and self-employment services to persons with disabilities. 
The RUMS is a comprehensive software solution to streamline and enhance the 
management of rehabilitation processes for individuals recovering from disabilities, or various medical 
conditions. RUMS integrates various functionalities to facilitate efficient communication, 
coordination, and monitoring of rehabilitation programs between healthcare professionals, therapists, 
and organizations. By integrating a range of comprehensive features, RUMS aims to optimize the entire 
continuum of care, from initial registration to ongoing treatment and progress monitoring. RUMS cater 
to the diverse needs of patients undergoing Speech Therapy (ST) and other rehabilitation therapies by 
offering tailored treatment plans. This system has features such as a Patient registration, Patient listing, 
Management dashboard, Consultation dashboard, Edit Consultation, Consultation Booking, View 
Consultation History, and View Details of Other Therapies, to further streamline operations and 
improve user experience. This system allows the patient to select a patient category from three options: 
Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Students, or General. Each category corresponds to a different 
registration amount. 
The core features of RUMS include Patient Information Management, Consultation 
booking, Treatment Planning and Scheduling, Progress Tracking and Reporting.

SYSTEM ANALYSIS 
2.1 EXISTING SYSTEM 
The Management of rehabilitation processes at Amar Jyothi Charitable Trust relied 
heavily on manual methods and disjointed systems. Healthcare professionals and therapists primarily 
utilized paper-based records, spreadsheets, and standalone software tools, resulting in fragmented data 
management and communication channels. Patient information was stored in physical files, making 
it challenging to access and update records efficiently. Treatment planning and scheduling were often 
done manually, leading to potential scheduling conflicts and inefficiencies in resource allocation. 
Individual patients were documented in disparate formats, making it difficult to track progress 
consistently across the rehabilitation unit. Healthcare professionals had to manually compile data from 
various sources, increasing the likelihood of errors and delays in decision-making processes. The lack 
of streamlined processes and communication channels resulted in inefficiencies and missed 
opportunities for enhancing patient care and outcomes. 
2.1.1 DRAWBACKS 
● Fragmented Data Management: Reliance on paper-based records, spreadsheets, and standalone 
software tools led to fragmented data management, making it difficult to consolidate and access 
patient information efficiently. 
● Limited Accessibility and Updates: Patient information stored in physical files hindered 
accessibility and timely updates, as healthcare professionals and therapists faced challenges in 
accessing and updating records promptly. 
● Manual Treatment Planning and Scheduling: Manual processes for treatment planning and 
scheduling increased the likelihood of errors, scheduling conflicts, and inefficiencies in resource 
allocation, impacting the delivery of timely and effective care to patients. 
● Manual Data Compilation: Healthcare professionals had to manually compile data from various 
sources, increasing the risk of errors and delays in decision-making processes, which could impact 
the quality and timeliness of patient care. 
● Lack of Real-Time Monitoring: Absence of integrated systems limited real-time monitoring of 
patient progress and treatment outcomes, making it challenging to identify and address issues 
promptly, and potentially delaying interventions to optimize patient outcomes.

4.2 Database 
4.2.1 MySQL  
MySQL is a high-performance, multi user relational database management system that is 
today the standard for database-driven software applications, both on and off the Web. Designed 
around three fundamental principles speed, stability, and ease of use and freely available under the 
GNU General Public License, MySQL has been dubbed “the world's most popular open-source 
database" by its parent company and with good reason. Official statistics reveal over five million sites 
are creating, using, and deploying MySQL based applications, with more coming into the fold on a 
daily basis. 
Characteristics of MySQL: 
● Reliability: MySQL is known for its reliability and stability. It offers features like data durability, 
crash recovery, and automatic backups to ensure that data remains accessible and intact even in the 
event of system failures or unexpected shutdowns. 
● High Availability: Built-in replication and automatic fail over ensure data accessibility even 
during failures. 
● Performance: MySQL is designed for high performance, capable of handling large volumes of 
data and concurrent user requests efficiently. 
● Scalability: It supports clustering, replication, and sharding to scale out database deployments and 
accommodate growing workloads.

4.3.1 JAVASCRIPT  
JavaScript is a very powerful client-side scripting language, introduced to “make web 
pages alive”. This allows us to create dynamic content for the web. JavaScript is a lightweight language 
that is open-source and allows cross-platform. It doesn’t require compilation and is interpreted with 
object-oriented capabilities. Also, it works with various other programming languages. And this is the 
reason for its vast use all around the world. Many popular websites and web applications like Google, 
Amazon, PayPal, etc use this language. The file extension of a JavaScript file is .js Most applications 
work due to an interaction between a client (user’s device) and a remote server. The client requests 
data from the server. The server receives the request, processes it and then responds accordingly. The

response sent back is in a user-readable format and is thus acceptable by the client. But this process 
takes time as well as resources. Although we usually need this connection, in some projects, JavaScript 
often helps to avoid it. JavaScript allows the validation of forms without the input of the server, 
reducing traffic. It provides wonderful tools for a more interactive and user-friendly website.  
Some of the basic functions of JavaScript are:   
● Auto complete: The search box gives suggestions, based on what the user has already typed.  
● Form validation: If the users make a mistake while filling a form, JavaScript immediately informs

● Fixes layout issues to avoid the overlapping of elements on the page.  
●  Adds animation to the page to make it more attractive.

●  Rich Interfaces - JavaScript provides numerous interfaces to make the web page more

4.3.2 Typescript 
Typescript is a free and open-source high-level programming language developed 
by Microsoft that adds static typing with optional type annotations to JavaScript. It is designed for the 
development of large applications and transpiled to JavaScript. Because Typescript is a superset of 
JavaScript, all JavaScript programs are syntactically valid Typescript, but they can fail to type
check for safety reasons. Typescript supports definition files that can contain type information of 
existing JavaScript libraries, much like C++ header files can describe the structure of existing object 
files. This enables other programs to use the values defined in the files as if they were statically typed 
Typescript entities.

PROJECT DESCRIPTION 
5.1 PROBLEM DEFINITION 
The Amar Jyoti Research and Rehabilitation Centre is currently relying on manual methods 
and disjointed systems for managing the rehabilitation processes. This has led to several challenges 
including fragmented data management, inefficient communication channels, and lack of streamlined 
processes. Healthcare professionals and therapists are struggling with paper-based records, 
spreadsheets, and standalone software tools, resulting in difficulties in accessing and updating patient 
information. Manual treatment planning and scheduling have led to potential conflicts and 
inefficiencies in resource allocation, while disparate formats for goal setting make it challenging to 
track progress consistently. The manual compilation of data from various sources also increases the 
likelihood of errors and delays in decision-making processes. Overall, the existing system is inefficient, 
prone to errors, and lacks the necessary coordination and communication channels required for 
effective rehabilitation management. 
5.2 PROJECT OVERVIEW 
The RUMS is a specialized software solution to address the shortcomings of the existing 
system at the Amar Jyoti Charitable Trust. RUMS aims to streamline and enhance the management of 
rehabilitation processes for individuals recovering from disabilities or medical conditions. By 
integrating various functionalities, RUMS will facilitate efficient communication, coordination, and 
monitoring of rehabilitation programs between healthcare professionals, therapists, and organizations. 
The core features of RUMS include Patient Information Management, Consultation booking, 
Treatment Planning and Scheduling, Progress Tracking, and Reporting. Additionally, RUMS will 
provide a centralized system for managing patient information, consultation history, booking 
appointments, treatment plans, and progress tracking, ensuring efficient data management and access. 
The system will also enable treatment planning for patients undergoing rehabilitation, with 
mechanisms to track progress over time and adjust treatment plans accordingly. Features such as 
Consultation Dashboard, Edit Consultation, Consultation Booking, View Consultation History, and 
View Details of Other Therapies will further streamline operations and improve user experience. 
Overall, RUMS aim to optimize the entire continuum of care, from initial registration to ongoing 
treatment and progress monitoring, catering to the diverse needs of patients undergoing rehabilitation 
at the Amar Jyoti Research and Rehabilitation Centre.

5.3 MODULES DESCRIPTION 
The following modules are: 
● Patient Registration 
● Consultation Booking 
● Speech Therapy 
● Management Dashboard 
5.3.1 Patient Registration  
RUMS is a robust patient registration and management system that categorizes 
patients into groups such as students, EWS, or general. It facilitates registration by capturing 
essential details like name, age, gender, and contact information, along with other necessary 
information. Each category may have different registration fees. 
Additionally, RUMS allows for easy listing of registered patients and provides functionality for 
editing patient details to ensure seamless management. Furthermore, it enables booking 
consultations for patients, offering various packages and allowing selection of consultants as per 
the patient's preference. 
5.3.2   Consultation Booking  
RUMS provide a comprehensive view of both open and closed consultations, 
showcasing patient details such as name, unit, consultation package, and consultant information. 
It also offers counts of open and closed patients for easy tracking. Users can filter closed 
consultations based on dates. 
Moreover, RUMS enables users to seamlessly manage patient care by adding new consultations 
through search functionalities based on first name, last name, or patient ID. Additionally, it 
facilitates the renewal of registrations for patients whose registrations have expired, ensuring 
continuous and efficient healthcare management. 
12 
5.3.3 Speech Therapy  
The Consultation Dashboard in RUMS empowers consultants to effectively manage 
consultations by providing visibility to both open and closed consultations, along with 
corresponding counts. It offers the flexibility to edit consultation and follow-up dates, enhancing 
the management of patient consultations and treatment plans. 
This module facilitates the addition of consultations through search, filter, and patient view 
functionalities, ensuring continuity of care and progress monitoring. Moreover, RUMS enables 
healthcare professionals to access consultation history for each patient, facilitating tracking of past 
interactions and treatment outcomes. 
With features like case sheets and case parameters and details of other therapies, RUMS promotes 
interdisciplinary collaboration and holistic patient care within the rehabilitation unit. Additionally, 
it allows for editing consultation details to accommodate changes as needed, ensuring seamless 
patient management. 
5.3.4 Management Dashboard 
The Management Dashboard in RUMS empowers management to oversee and 
manage various aspects of the rehabilitation units efficiently. It allows for the addition and editing 
of rehab units, consultants, consultation packages, and areas, facilitating streamlined operations 
and resource management. 
Furthermore, the dashboard enables management to view consultation details by searching patients 
via first name, last name, or patient ID. It also provides the capability to generate reports on patient 
registrations for each category (EWS, student, general) in charts by month, week, and year-wise, 
offering valuable insights into patient demographics and trends. Additionally, the module allows 
for the generation of reports on closed consultations for each rehab unit in charts by month, week, 
and year, aiding in monitoring the utilization and performance of each unit. It also displays a list 
of closed consultations for reference and review purposes, ensuring comprehensive oversight of 
patient care processes.

SYSTEM TESTING 
As part of the system testing process for RUMS, I followed a systematic approach to evaluate the 
accuracy and performance of the system. Here's an overview of the steps undertook: 
6.1  UNIT TESTING 
Unit testing is a crucial aspect of the development process for the RUMS. It involves 
testing individual components or units of code to ensure they function correctly and reliably. 
Thorough unit testing for the Patient Registration, Consultation Booking, Treatment Planning and 
Scheduling, Progress Tracking and Reporting, and Consultation Dashboard and History modules 
of the RUMS is essential to ensure its functionality, security, and efficiency. Test cases encompass 
various scenarios such as successful patient registration, error handling for missing fields, 
uniqueness of patient identification numbers, validation of input field formats, booking and 
cancellation of consultations. Through comprehensive testing, the RUMS system can meet user 
requirements and expectations, providing reliable support for rehabilitation processes while 
safeguarding patient information and ensuring smooth communication and coordination among

Centre. By transitioning from manual methods and disjointed systems to a comprehensive software 
solution, RUMS streamlines communication, coordination, and monitoring across healthcare 
professionals, therapists, and administrative staff. The centralized system for managing patient 
information, consultation history, treatment plans, and progress tracking ensures efficient data 
management and access, ultimately leading to improved patient care and outcomes. RUMS offers a 
range of features including patient registration, management dashboard, consultation dashboard, 
consultation booking, and viewing consultation history, all aimed at optimizing the continuum of 
care for individuals recovering from disabilities or medical conditions. Treatment planning and 
scheduling modules enable personalized and effective care, with mechanisms to track progress over 
time and adjust treatment plans accordingly. The successful implementation of RUMS demonstrates 
the commitment of Amar Jyoti Research and Rehabilitation Centre to leveraging technology for the 
betterment of patient rehabilitation.
